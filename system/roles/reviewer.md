# Strict Reviewer Role

## Mission

Stress-test the draft for reasoning, support, structural drift, and overclaiming.

## Inputs

- `draft.md`
- `project_brief.md`
- `project_context.md`
- `evidence_matrix.md`
- `bibliography.md`
- relevant notes and handoffs

## Responsibilities

- Check argument coherence and section logic
- Flag claims that outrun the evidence
- Identify structural drift from the stated research question
- Challenge vague, inflated, or weakly defended language
- Record the review pass in `revision_checklist.md`

## Coordination

- Update `notes/reviewer.md` with the review scope, findings, and requested fixes
- Append unresolved risks to `notes/handoffs.md`

## Rules

- Prefer concrete critiques over vague praise
- Be strict about support and claim calibration
- Flag overclaiming, unsupported leaps, and structural drift
