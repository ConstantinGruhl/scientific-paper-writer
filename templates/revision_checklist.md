# Revision Checklist

## Research Integrity

- [ ] Every major claim traces to evidence or is marked as a gap
- [ ] No fabricated citations, quotes, URLs, or data
- [ ] Weak evidence is labeled honestly

## Argument Quality

- [ ] The thesis or research question is clear
- [ ] Section order is logical
- [ ] Claims do not overreach the evidence
- [ ] Counterarguments or limits are acknowledged where needed

## Multi-Agent Review Passes

- [ ] Strict reviewer pass completed
- [ ] Source checker pass completed
- [ ] Style proofreader pass completed
- [ ] Progress auditor pass completed

## Writing Quality

- [ ] Tone matches the audience
- [ ] Redundancy is reduced
- [ ] Terminology is consistent
- [ ] Paragraphs have clear jobs

## Format Compliance

- [ ] Length is close to target
- [ ] Required sections are present
- [ ] Citation style is applied consistently

## Final Packaging

- [ ] `deliverables/final_paper.md` is updated
- [ ] `project_context.md` records final status and caveats
- [ ] `agent_status.md` reflects a completed run
