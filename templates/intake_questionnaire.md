# Intake Questionnaire

Use these questions when a new paper request is underspecified.

## Minimum Viable Questions

1. What is the theme or topic of the paper?
2. What scientific or academic context should the assistant know?
3. What type of paper is this?
4. What format do you need?
5. How long should it be?
6. What is the main focus, thesis, or research question?
7. Who is the audience or target venue?
8. What citation style should be used?

## High-Value Follow-Ups

9. Do you already have required sources, papers, or datasets?
10. Are there sources, journals, or authors that must be included?
11. Are there sources that should be avoided?
12. What sections are required?
13. Is there a deadline or milestone?
14. What level of technical depth is expected?
15. Are you arguing a position, reviewing literature, reporting results, or proposing a framework?

## Stop Rule

Stop asking questions once the missing answers no longer change the research and drafting strategy in a material way.
